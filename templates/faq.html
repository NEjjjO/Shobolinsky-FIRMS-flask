Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is this application for?
This application helps report and track fire incidents in real-time.
How do I report a fire?
You can report a fire by clicking on the "Report a Fire" button and then clicking on the map at the fire location.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously and ensure that your information is secure.
Who can see the reported fires?
The reported fires are visible to the public and the relevant authorities for prompt action.
Can I edit or delete a fire report?
Currently, users cannot edit or delete fire reports once submitted. Please ensure the accuracy of the information before reporting.
